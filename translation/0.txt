A chaotic difficulty that shifts gameplay towards more combat and carnage. There are new monster spawns, higher tier enemies appear earlier and are stronger and more aggressive than usual. However, you also get the weapons, consumables and ammo required to put them down. You also have the chance to encounter a couple of companions along the way.
一个狂乱的难度，游戏性会趋向更多的战斗和杀戮。有更多新怪物出现，更早出现高等级的敌人，而且比平时更强大，更有攻击性。然而，你也能得到打倒它们所需的武器、消耗品和弹药。一路上你还有机会遇到几个同伴。
